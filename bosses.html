    Additional information
    Strategy

    It cannot move and always turns to target players in base radius.

    As long as the player is in the base, the eye casts a strong laser aimed at players, which can be dodged by rolling or flying upwards using a mount.

    If no players are in the base, the laser will be aimed at your pals. It is immune to ground-based pal moves and can only be hurt on its hands and head eye.

    Killing the hands and forehead releases True Eyes of Cthulhu which have their own powerful attacks. After hands and forehead are dead, the chest (heart) opens up.

    Killing the heart defeats the boss.
